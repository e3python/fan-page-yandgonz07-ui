The Crisp Taste of Sprite

Sprite is a clear, lemon-lime flavored soft drink created by the Coca-Cola Company.

Different variations flavors

  • Original Sprite
  • Cranberry Sprite
  • Tropical Mix Sprite
  • Lymonade Sprite
  • Sprite Chill

    • Origin of Sprite

    Sprite was created by The Coca-Cola Company in 1961 as a response to the growing popularity of lemon-lime flavored sodas. It quickly became one of the most popular soft drinks worldwide due to its crisp and refreshing taste.
